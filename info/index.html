VJO-D design methodology

design methodology for making design decisions more precise, verifiable and less accidental.

VJO-D is a personal working methodology i developed for my own design practice. it is built around three requirements: a design decision should be verifiable, justified and outcome-driven. before shaping the solution, the expected result, reasoning and evaluation criteria are defined upfront.

the methodology describes the way i structure my own design work. it makes the process clearer for the team, reduces arbitrary revisions and keeps decisions connected to meaning, purpose and expected effect. the project becomes easier to manage because design is discussed through the task, the reasoning and the result.
